About Us

🌐Mission

Our mission is to create a virtual notice board 📋 that will enable our users to have a perfect campus life 🏫 by striking a balance ⚖️ between academics 📚 and extracurricular activities 🎭.
Our platform will not only unify students of the same branches 🌿 but will also bring together all students, regardless of their course or year of study 🎓. We aim to foster a sense of connectivity 🤝 among students from all branches.
Campulse will revolutionize 🔄 the interconnections between college departments 🏢, fueling collaborations 🤝, events 🎉, and the development of all kinds of skill sets 💻📈 that are essential in today’s professional world 🌎.

🌟Vision

Our vision is to create a self-sustaining platform 🔗 that will connect not only the students of our college 🏫 but also students from all the colleges in our city 🌆. We are dedicated to building a service accessible to every student 📲, ensuring that no one misses out on valuable opportunities 🚀 to upgrade their skill set 📈 or to learn something new 📚.

📋What do we do?

Campulse is designed to provide a platfrom:

  • To connect all the students of our campus.
  • To make it 'the one place' when looking for new events and opportunities.
  • For club managers to promote their events.

Campulse’s Events section🎉 keeps all students updated on ongoing and upcoming events 📅 from all the clubs they’re interested in. Never miss out on a chance to participate, learn, and grow! 🌟

Our Notes section aims to eliminate the last-minute scramble for study materials 📚 before exams. No more begging friends and seniors for lab reports, class notes, or past exam questions – everything you need is right here! 📖✨

Have you ever lost something on campus 🧢 and struggled to get it back? Or perhaps you’ve found something that belongs to someone else 👜? With Campulse’s Lost and Found section, you can upload details of lost or found items, making it easy for the owner to retrieve them! 🗂️🤝

A single place to remain updated, organized and connected to your campus!
