📚 Book Reading Competition: Explore, Engage, Excel !

Dear Readers 🔊
As you all know October 15th is celebrated as Vachan Prerna Divas or Reading Day to honor the birth anniversary of Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, the Avishkar Club proudly presents the Book Reading Competition.
Interested readers have to make a short video of 4-5 minutes on the book read by him or her and submit the video recording on the following Google Form by 14th October 2024.

✔ Guidelines:
1. Choose any book—fiction, non-fiction, or technical—that intrigues you.
2. Read it thoroughly and absorb its essence.
3. Prepare a summary or review video.
4. Upload it on a Google Form.

✔ Who can participate?
• Students from any discipline and year
• Faculty and staff members (teaching and non-teaching)
🏆 Let words ignite your imagination! Join us for a literary adventure.
